Exceptional experience

Our team cannot say enough positive statements about this budget hotel in Thamel. The rooms are clean and comfortable with plenty of hot water and cool air conditioning. More importantly the staff was amazing. I recently traveled with a group of teens for a week and the hotel staff could not have been more accommodating. The restaurant was affordable, delicious and Chef Dhakal provided us with five-star quality meals custom made to our specifications. No request wast to great and he was constantly offering to do more. During our visit we had two student birthdays. No only did he prepare us an elegant meal, chef spent the day baking a most wonderful birthday cake- then the entire hotel staff joined him in singing "happy birthday" to the team- you could tell that they were as happy we were when presenting the cake to the birthday guests. From the front desk staff to the gate security every member of the team made it their personal mission to ensure our comfort and safety. The hotel manager checked in with me daily to ensure our satisfaction. On our day of departure my entire team felt as though we were saying goodby to new friends. I would absolutely stay here again and recommend this hotel to friends. The hotel is located in the heart of Thamel so we could walk to many attractions including local markets, shopping and an epic rock wall.
